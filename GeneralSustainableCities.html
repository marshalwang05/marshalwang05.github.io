Goal 11 - Sustainable Cities

Sustainable Cities also known as ecocities, can be seen all over the world....A sustaianble city focuses on
a) Reducing heat, water and air pollution
b) Designed so current population's living needs will not compromise a future one's
c) Applies sustainable living practices and energy sources

Cities like these are the only way to sustain ourselves in the future. Global warming is a real and serious issue that must be adressed via creating places that can sustain human life in large numbers alongside a welcome enviroment for native ecosytems.


ustainable cities can be found all over the world spanning all the way from Copenhagen, Denmark to the San Francisco, US to Singapore. All these have one thing in common, their energy efficiency in buildings, general cleanliness and motivation for a greener enviroment. For instance, in 2014. Singapore had a CO2 emission of only 10.2 metric tons in comparison to Toronto's 49.2 despite having a population of near double Toronto's. With this, if every country in the world strived to become more like these sustainable cities. The world would be a far different one than the one we see today.
